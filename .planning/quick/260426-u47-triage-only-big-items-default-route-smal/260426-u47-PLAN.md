<objective>
Restructure the ingest pipeline so small / metadata-only items skip Stage 1 (the relevance gate) and go straight to Stage 2, where Stage 2 itself can produce a final action (auto-file or auto-ignore) without human triage when claude is confident enough. Adds a routing decision at ingest time, a `decision` field to Stage 2 output, a cold-start guard, and a new terminal `filed` status.

Purpose: Stage 1 is expensive (claude reads file content). Most items are small enough that the metadata block alone is sufficient signal — they shouldn't pay the Stage 1 token cost. And once Stage 2 says "this is junk" or "this is certain across all 3 axes with vocabulary I already know," the human shouldn't be in the loop either.

Output: routing logic in `/api/ingest`, extended Stage 2 schema + prompt, terminal-state transitions + cold-start guard in `/api/classify`, new `FILED` status constant, focused tests on the new branches.
</objective>
